Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

A classic Italian dish that's perfect for family dinners. It combines delicious spaghetti with a hearty ground beef sauce, topped with Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 800g can of tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • A handful of fresh basil, chopped
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Then drain and set aside.
  2. While the spaghetti is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and saut√© until the onion is translucent.
  3. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until it's browned and no longer pink. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Pour in the tomato sauce and stir well. Let the sauce simmer for about 15 minutes, or until it has thickened.
  5. Stir in the chopped basil and cook for another 2 minutes.
  6. Combine the cooked spaghetti with the sauce, stirring until the spaghetti is well coated.
  7. Serve the spaghetti bolognese in plates or bowls, and top with a generous sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese.
