Chicken Parmesan

Description

This delicious dish features crispy breaded chicken covered with flavorful tomato sauce and melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • A few fresh basil leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Dip each chicken breast in the beaten eggs, then coat in bread crumbs. Make sure each piece is fully covered.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook until it's golden and crispy on each side. This should take about 3-4 minutes per side.
  4. Once the chicken is cooked, cover each piece with the marinara sauce, then top with mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  5. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly, which should take about 20 minutes.
  6. Garnish the chicken Parmesan with fresh basil leaves and serve with your choice of side (pasta, salad, etc).
